# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

All BePower team members and contributors are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional in all interactions
- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the team and the project
- Take responsibility for mistakes and learn from them

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Disruptive or unprofessional conduct

## Enforcement

Violations should be reported to your team lead or HR. All reports will be handled confidentially.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all BePower repositories, communication channels, and team interactions.
